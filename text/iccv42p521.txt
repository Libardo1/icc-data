ORDER SETTING AWARDS SUBJECT TO OFFSETS

Upon the findings of fact and opinion this day entered herein, and
which are hereby made a part of this order,

IT IS ORDERED that (subject to allowable offsets, if any):

1. The Peoria plaintiff in Docket 313, on behalf of the Kaakaskia,
is awarded $7,142,250 as additional compensation for the Kaskaskia's 1803

cession of Royce Area 48;

2. The Potawatomi plaintiffs in Docket 15-D, 29-3, and 311 are

awarded $307,046 as additional compensation for the Potawatomi's 1818
cession of Royce Area 98;

3. The Peoria plaintiff in Docket 314-A, on behalf of the Wes, is
awarded $377,874 as additional compensation for the Wea's 1818 cession of
Royce Area 98 and Tract H of Royce Area 180;

4. The Kickapoo plaintiffs in Docket 315 are awarded $11,427,130 as
additional compensation for the Kickapoo's 1819 cession of Royce Area 110;

5. This matter shall now proceed to a determination of the amount
of offsets, if any, to which the defendant is entitled.
