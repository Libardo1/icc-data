Upon consideration of the petitions filed herein by the plaintiffs
and by witness E. 0. Fuller on August 6, 1958, the objections and comments of the defendant and the Bureau of Indian Affairs on November 12,
l958, and other documents in the record including objections of the plaintiff tribe, and after an oral hearing on said matters on December 8, l958

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED AND ADJUDGED that from the award of $416,240.85

2. For reimbursable costs and expenses of the litigation paid or
incurred for plaintiffs, the following:

To E. L. Crawford as reimbursement of expenses incurred in the
litigation by him $l645.30, $1270.30 of which amount is a reimbursement
of expenses advanced by him, and $375.00 of which is to pay for services
rendered by Dorothy Crawford.

the sum of $5,000.00.

fund on deposit with him is approved.
