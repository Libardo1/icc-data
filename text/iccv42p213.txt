ORDER GRANTING IN PART, DENYING IN PART DEFENDANT'S MOTION

UPON CONSIDERATION of the defendant's motion to enter adjustments in
the valuation award, filed September 2, 1977, and plaintiffs‘ answer thereto,
filed January 12, 1978, and for the reasons stated in the opinion accompanying this order, the Commission is of the opinion that defendant's motion

should be granted in part and denied in part.

IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED that defendant's motion be, and the same
hereby is, granted to the extent that the value of plaintiffs‘ interest
in the Sioux Fort Laramie lands, acquired by the United States under the
Treaty of April 29, 1868, 15 Stat. 635, shall be adjusted to a sum of

$23,054,700.00.

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that in all other respects defendant's motion
be, and the same hereby is, denied.

Dated at Washington, D. C., this 19th day of July 1978
