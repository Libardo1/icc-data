FINAL AWARD

An interlocutory order was entered herein on August 10, 1978,
42 Ind. Cl. Comm. 298, awarding the plaintiffs and intervenors $566,024.21,

less such gratuitous offsets as defendant may be entitled under the
provisions of the Indian Claims Commission Act. On August 14, 1978,
counsel for defendant notified the Commission that, in order to expedite
the entry of a final award herein, defendant will reserve for other
docketed cases involving these plaintiffs and intervenors any claim

for gratuitous offsets against the interlocutory award herein. In such
circumstances, the Commission will, upon motion sue sponte, enter a final
award herein.

IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED that as a final award in full satisfaction
of all claims against the defendant in Docket 29-D, plaintiffs and
intervenors do have and recover from defendant on behalf of the
Potawatomi Tribe, as constituted in 1805, the sum of five hundred
sixty-six thousand twenty—four dollars and twenty-one cents ($566,024.21),
