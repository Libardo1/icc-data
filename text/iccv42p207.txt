ORDER VACATING CERTIFICATION AND TRANSFER
TO THE UNITED STATES COURT OF CLAIMS

The above-captioned case is included in our order certifying and
transferring cases to the United States Court of Claims dated July 13,
1978 (42 Ind. Cl. Com. 202). It appearing that evidence in this
case may be used in the consolidated proceeding in Dockets 279—C and
250—A and that it is not practical to identify and duplicate such

evidence,

IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED that the order certifying and transferring
cases to the United States Court of Claims dated July 13, 1978 (42 Ind.

Cl. Comm. 202) be, and the same hereby is, vacated with regard to Docket
279—D, and

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the Clerk of the Commission shall file
a copy of this order with the Clerk of the United States Court of Claims.

Dated at Washington, D. c., this 17th day of July 1978
