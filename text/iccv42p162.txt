FINAL AWARD

Upon plaintiffs’ motion of June 1, 1978, for entry of final award,
to which motion no objections have been filed, and defendant having
waived its right to claim gratuitous offsets chargeable against the
interlocutory award previously entered herein,

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that, as a final award in full satisfaction
of all claims against the defendant in Docket 59, plaintiffs do have
and recover from the defendant on behalf of Saginaw Chippewa Band of
Indians as constituted in 1808, the sum of three million four hundred
seventy—nine thousand three hundred eight dollars ($3,479,308.00).

Dated at Washington, D. C., this 22nd day of June 1978.
